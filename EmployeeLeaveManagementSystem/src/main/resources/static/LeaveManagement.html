⚠ Timezone Note for Global Teams
The scheduler runs at UTC midnight. Employees in timezones ahead of UTC (e.g. IST +5:30, JST +9) will see their leave status update before their workday begins, which is correct. Teams in timezones behind UTC (e.g. PST -8) may see the update partway through their previous day. Consider adding a zone parameter per-employee if your team spans many timezones.
The scheduler performs two tasks each run:
1. Marks employees ON_LEAVE for approved leaves starting today
2. Marks employees back to ACTIVE for leaves that have ended
